Services We Offer

We provide a range of services for your pets including grooming, training, and veterinary care. Each service is tailored to ensure the well-being and happiness of your pet. Below are the details, pricing, and benefits for each service.

Pricing

Grooming

Description: Professional grooming services to keep your pet looking their best. This includes bathing, brushing, and nail trimming.

Pricing: $50 per session

Benefits: Keeps your pet clean, reduces shedding, and prevents matting.

Training

Description: Comprehensive training programs to help your pet learn obedience and behavioral skills.

Pricing: $100 for a 5-session package

Benefits: Enhances your pet's behavior and strengthens your bond with them.

Veterinary Care

Description: Health check-ups and medical care provided by certified veterinarians.

Pricing: $75 per consultation

Benefits: Ensures your pet is healthy and up-to-date with vaccinations.
